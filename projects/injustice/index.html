About

This is a two player game where players takes turn matching cards to recruit characters to battle after player two matches.

  • The player who takes their opponent's health to zero first wins.
  • Player's turn ends when a pair of card is matched or 3 failed attempts are made.
  • If player passes turn because of 3 failed attempts, that player will lose 1 health point.
  • Card's strength is predetermined.
  • Enjoy and have fun!
